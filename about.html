My interest in visual work started with photography and animation, where I became fascinated by the process of capturing moments and building narratives through images. Over time, that curiosity evolved into a deeper interest in graphic design and visual communication.

While studying New Media Design and Web Development at BCIT, I developed skills across design, video editing, and digital media. That experience helped me understand how design, motion, and storytelling can work together to create engaging visual experiences.

As a designer, I tend to notice the small details — how layouts guide the eye, how colour affects mood, and how motion can add meaning to a visual idea. I enjoy designing work that feels intentional and human rather than overly rigid or corporate.

I’m particularly interested in projects that combine design with storytelling, whether through branding, illustration, digital media, or motion graphics.

I’m currently continuing to develop my portfolio and looking for opportunities to grow as a designer. I’m especially interested in roles that allow me to work on creative visual projects, collaborate with others, and keep learning new ways to communicate ideas through design.
